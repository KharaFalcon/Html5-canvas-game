Fish Game Instructions

The aim of the game

  • The aim of the game is to collect as many coins as you before you are caught by the sharks
  • The more coins you collect the better you do
  • During the game the sharks will get faster so beware!
  • When you reach a certain point in the game there will be a bonus level. Try to collect as many coins as you can because you are going against the clock.

Instructions

  1. To start press the start button located below the game screen
  2. The player will spawn into the game as a orange/yellow fish.
  3. Use the mouse or touch to control where the fish goes on the screen.
  4. The Sharks will start moving from left to right. Try to avoid them they will cause damage.
  5. You have three lives overall.
  6. The coins are displayed as yellow cricles and will fall from above. These will improve your score.
  7. During the game there will be an opportunity to gain a life by collecting the health package which will be floating down the screen as a purple cirlce.
  8. Your points and damage will appear at the top of the screen.
  9. To change the screen size for a better experience press the full screen button below the game screen.
  10. To reduce it back to normal size press the reduce button next to it.
  11. The game plays music and has sound effects such as a hit sound when a collision occurs. You can mute these using the button provided.
