Fish Game Instructions

Welcome to Fishin' for Gold! Your goal is to collect as many coins as possible while avoiding the sharks.

The Aim of the Game

  • Collect as many coins as you can before being caught by the sharks.
  • The more coins you collect, the higher your score.
  • As the game progresses, sharks will get faster—so beware!
  • Reach a certain score to unlock a bonus level where you race against the clock.

How to Play

  1. Press the Start Game button below the game screen.
  2. You will control an orange/yellow fish.
  3. Use your mouse or touchscreen to move the fish.
  4. Sharks will move from left to right—avoid them as they cause damage.
  5. You have three lives in total.
  6. Coins, displayed as yellow circles, will fall from above—collect them to improve your score.
  7. A purple circle (health package) will float down—collect it to restore one life.
  8. Your score and health will be displayed at the top of the screen.
  9. For a better experience, press the Full Screen button below the game screen.
  10. To return to normal size, press the Reduce Screen button.
  11. The game includes background music and sound effects. You can mute them using the Mute Sound button.
